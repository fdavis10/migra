WHAT REFUGEE STATUS IS

Refugee status allows a foreign national to legally remain in Russia, receive protection, social guarantees, and access to basic government services. The decision on refugee recognition is made following the submission of a petition, completion of a questionnaire, an interview, and verification of all circumstances. A refugee certificate is issued, which confirms identity and the right to remain.

ADVANTAGES

Right to temporary residence in Russia on lawful grounds.
Protection against expulsion to a country where the person faces danger.
Access to medical care on equal terms with Russian citizens.
Ability to work and engage in entrepreneurial activity.
Social protection and social security.
Right to children's education at state educational institutions.
Receipt of a travel document for departure from and return to the Russian Federation.
Ability to apply for permanent residence or Russian citizenship where grounds exist.

WHO MAY APPLY

Foreign nationals and stateless persons who are outside their country of citizenship;
Who have well-founded fear of becoming victims of persecution on grounds of race, religion, nationality, political opinion, or membership in a particular social group;
Who are unable or unwilling to use the protection of their country due to these fears;
Stateless persons who were outside their country of former residence for the same reasons.

GROUNDS

Confirmed facts of persecution or threat to life/health on racial, religious, national, political, or social grounds.
Inability or unwillingness to return to the country of citizenship or former residence due to threat of persecution.
Absence of grounds for refusal (e.g., commission of serious crimes contrary to UN principles).

Important:

Refugee status is not granted for economic reasons or due to natural disasters.
The petition is submitted in person or through a representative to authorized Ministry of Internal Affairs bodies of Russia.
The decision is made following individual consideration of all circumstances of the case.

HOW “RESIDENT” HELPS

We help formulate the circumstances of the case, prepare documents and explanations, and accompany interaction with the authorities throughout the process.

LIMITATIONS

Contradictions in statements or incomplete explanations complicate review of the application; absence of evidence of persecution may lead to refusal.

PROCEDURE

Consultation — preparation of the application and evidence — filing with the competent body — interview if required — review — decision.

DOCUMENTS

Application for refugee status; identity documents if any; materials supporting persecution or danger; translations as required.

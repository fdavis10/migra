WHAT TRP IS

A temporary residence permit (TRP) is an official status that allows a foreign national to legally reside and work in Russia for 3 years. The TRP is the first full-fledged stage of legalization and a mandatory step on the path to obtaining a residence permit and Russian citizenship.

The TRP is issued as a stamp in the passport or as a separate document and grants the right to settle in a specific region of Russia.

ADVANTAGES OF THE SERVICE

Obtaining a TRP opens a wide range of opportunities:

official employment without a patent;
right to medical care at state institutions;
ability to register as a sole proprietor or self-employed;
access to education for children;
ability to apply for a residence permit after just 8 months;
legal long-term residence without the risk of deportation.

This is a full transition from temporary stay to a stable legal status.

WHO CAN OBTAIN TRP

Foreign nationals who:

have received a quota;
are married to a Russian citizen;
have relatives who are Russian citizens;
are studying in Russia;
have other lawful grounds.

GROUNDS

TRP quota; marriage to a Russian citizen; education in Russia; presence of relatives; participation in government programs.

HOW “RESIDENT” HELPS

Migration service “Resident” provides full process support:

document verification;
package preparation;
assistance with the medical examination;
appointment scheduling at the Ministry of Internal Affairs;
accompaniment during submission;
assistance in case of refusal.

LIMITATIONS

Absence of a quota; violations of migration regulations; criminal record; medical conditions; false information.

PROCEDURE

Document collection
Application submission
Medical examination
Awaiting decision (up to 60 days)
Receipt of TRP

DOCUMENTS

Passport, application, medical certificates, language certificate, proof of income, registration.
